## How to Use

1. Select your preferred test duration or word limit from the top menu.
2. Click on the text area or press `Tab` + `Enter` to focus and restart.
3. Start typing! The timer will begin automatically on your first keystroke.
4. View your detailed performance analytics immediately after completion.

---

## How Metrics Are Calculated

The application uses standard formulas to ensure industry-accurate tracking:

*   **Gross WPM:** 
    $$\text{WPM} = \frac{\text{Total Characters Typed} / 5}{\text{Time Spent in Minutes}}$$
*   **Net WPM:** 
    $$\text{Net WPM} = \text{Gross WPM} - \frac{\text{Uncorrected Errors}}{\text{Time Spent in Minutes}}$$
*   **Accuracy:** 
    $$\text{Accuracy } (\%) = \left( \frac{\text{Correct Characters}}{\text{Total Keystrokes}} \right) \times 100$$
